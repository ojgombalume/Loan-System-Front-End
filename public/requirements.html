Loan Requirements

What you need to apply for a loan

Required Documents

  • Valid National ID (Omang)

    A clear copy of your national identification card

  • Proof of Employment

    Recent payslip(1 month validity)

    Employment letter

  • Bank Statements

    Last 3 months of bank statements

Eligibility Criteria

Age Requirement

Must be between 21 and 65 years old

Employment Status

Must be in permanent employment or employed with verifiable income

Citizenship

Must be a Botswana citizen or permanent resident

Credit History

Must have a good credit standing with no defaults

Additional Requirements

  • Minimum monthly income of P2,000
  • Must have been employed for at least 6 months
  • Debt-to-income ratio should not exceed 50%
  • Must provide verifiable contact details
  • Must provide next of kin information

For Married Applicants

If married in community of property, spouse consent and documentation will be required including:

  • Spouse's ID/Omang
  • Spouse's signature on the loan agreement
  • Marriage certificate (optional)

Ready to Apply?

If you meet all the requirements above, you can proceed with your loan application
